:Caveat: The metric the evaluations produce must be bounded which is typical for a 
         lot of the metrics in NLP e.g. :math:`[0,1]` for accuracy, recall, precision, 
         and F1. This caveat is to ensure strong theoretical guarantees of choosing the 
         best model 
         (`Karnin et al. 2013 <http://proceedings.mlr.press/v28/karnin13.pdf>`_).
